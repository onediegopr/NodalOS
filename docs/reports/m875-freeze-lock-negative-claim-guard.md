# M875 - Freeze Lock Negative Claim Guard

Project: NODAL OS.

The guard rejects false interpretations of the freeze lock:

- Freeze lock does not mean public release ready.
- Freeze lock does not mean Chrome Web Store ready.
- Freeze lock does not mean signed ZIP ready.
- Freeze lock does not mean runtime productive enabled.
- Freeze lock does not mean provider/cloud enabled.
- Freeze lock does not mean filesystem write enabled.
- Freeze lock does not mean browser automation enabled.
- Freeze lock does not mean capability unlock enabled.
- Freeze lock does not mean product files may be modified.
- Freeze lock does not mean Bridge/CSP may be modified.
- Freeze lock does not mean future audits are no longer required.
- Freeze lock does not mean deferred findings F3/F5/F6/F7/F9 are fixed.
- Freeze lock does not mean real runtime re-entry is approved.
